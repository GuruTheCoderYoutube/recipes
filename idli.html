Idlis

Idli is one of the most healthy and popular South Indian breakfast dish. They are soft, light, fluffy.

Ingredients

:

  1. 1 cup regular rice + 1 cup parboiled rice or 2 cups idli rice or 2 cups parboiled rice
  2. ½ cup whole or split urad dal – 120 grams whole or split urad dal (husked black gram)
  3. ¼ cup thick poha – 20 grams (flattened rice)
  4. ¼ teaspoon fenugreek seeds (methi seeds)
  5. 2 cups water – for soaking rice
  6. 1 cup water – for soaking uraddal
  7. ½ cup water – for grinding urad dal or add as required
    8. ¾ to 1 cup water – for grinding rice or add as required 1 teaspoon rock salt (edible and food grade) or sea salt oil – as required to apply to the idli moulds 2 to 2.5 cups water – for steaming idli

Instructions

:

  1. Soak Rice and Lentils.
  2. Grind or Blend Rice and Lentils.
  3. Ferment Batter
  4. Then pour the batter into idli-shaped contains inside vessels.
  5. And place in a cooker and steam/cook it for 15 minutes.
