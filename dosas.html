Dosas

A dosa is a thin batter based dish(usually crispy) originating from South India, made from a fermented batter predominantly consisting of lentils and rice.

Ingredients

:

  1. ½ cup idli rice or parboiled rice or regular rice – 100 grams
  2. ½ cup regular rice – 100 grams
  3. ¼ cup urad dal – 50 grams (husked whole or split black gram)
  4. ⅛ teaspoon fenugreek seeds or 2 pinches (methi seeds or methi dana)
  5. 2 tablespoons thick poha (flattened rice)
  6. 1.5 cups water – for soaking both rice and lentils
  7. ¾ cup water – for grinding, do add water as required
  8. ½ teaspoon rock salt edible and food grade or non-iodized salt or sea salt crystals or Himalayan pink salt
  9. oil as required

Instructions

:

  1. Soaking rice and lentils
  2. Grind or blend the soaked ingredients
  3. Ferment Dosa Batter
  4. First put some oil on a pan, and heat it.
  5. Put the dosa batter on the pan and spread it such that it becomes the shape of the circle.
  6. After 5 minutes flip the dosa.
  7. Then do the same for the other side.
  8. And take it out the pan.
